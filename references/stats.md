## Product line specifications

### Flowline Plus — CPVC (hot/cold water plumbing)

- NSF/ANSI 14 & 61 certified
- Designed for 50-year service life
- Non-toxic, non-corrosive, no bacterial growth

### Greenline — UPVC (cold water systems)

- Available up to 8″ diameter
- Schedule 40 and Schedule 80 configurations
- **Cold water only — rated to 60°C maximum.** Not for hot water systems.
- ISI certified, lead-free compound, UV-stable and weather-resistant
- Designed for 50-year service life
- "Granule Technology" for leak-proof fitting joints
- Full system: pipes, fittings, ball valves, flanges, unions, clamps, solvent cements, primers

### Drainline — SWR (soil, waste, rainwater drainage)

- 100% ISI certified
- Virgin, UV-resistant, high-rigidity PVC compound
- **DM-seal jointing system** — factory-fit seals, pre-chamfered, for leak-proof installation
- Ring-fit and solvent-fit jointing options
- Manufactured via twin-screw extrusion (pipes) and injection molding (fittings)
- Self-extinguishing, chemical-resistant, rust/corrosion-proof

### Terraline — UDS (underground drainage systems)

- Sizes: **110mm, 160mm, 200mm**
- **Foam core technology**
- Chemical resistance: pH 2 to pH 12
- Temperature tolerance: 40°C constant flow, 60°C short-term flow
- Ringfit jointing with factory-fit DM-Seal
- Fully compatible with the Drainline system for combined internal/external drainage

### Agriline (irrigation / agricultural pipes)

- Virgin, UV-stabilized, high-strength PVC compound — **imported compound from USA**
- NSF/ANSI 14 & 61 certified via IAPMO India
- **Temperature resistance up to 93°C**
- Designed for 50-year service life
- **Pressure rating up to 25% higher than standard alternatives**
- **Impact strength 3x more resistant than generic CPVC**
- Chlorine-resistant, no scaling or pitting
- Storage: max stack height 1.5m, single diameter per stack, keep out of direct sunlight

### Deepline — Column & Casing Pipes (borewell)

- **Screw Lock System** — eliminates coupler loosening and column slipping
- High-pressure orientation technique — improved drop and notch impact strength
- **~30% higher water output** (lower C-factor, no scaling/electrolytic deposition)
- Rubber O-rings absorb pump vibration, prevent leakage
- Square thread design with high-impact formulation
- Applications: submersible/jet pump borewells, irrigation, chemical distribution, industrial mining, domestic use
